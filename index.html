Mobius Trip is a one-hour, completely unscripted improv comedy show where a single audience story explodes into an entire universe of characters, twists, and unexpected connections.

Performed by improv duo Kartik Shanbhag and Felix Hartley, the show unfolds live on stage as they build multiple storylines and characters on the spot — no script, no planning, just pure improvisation.

Expect fast-paced comedy, strange coincidences, and a touch of sci-fi weirdness as timelines bend and stories loop back on themselves. With a combined 10+ years of experience in improv comedy and theatre, Kartik and Felix turn one simple audience suggestion into a hilarious, unpredictable theatrical ride.

After two successful shows across Bangalore with 100+ audience members, Mobius Trip returns for its third outing.

Catch the show April 4th at Kadhaigal, Sahakarnagar — and who knows, your story might start the trip.
